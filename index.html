Thank You For Reading

"Thank You For Reading" Before u finish reading this, i want to ask you something. after reading everything i wrote, i would like to know your perspective about me. Not only the good things, but also the things you think i need to improve. i want to know how you see me as a person, based on everything you have experienced with me. What do you think are my strengths? What are my weaknesses? What things about me make you feel comfortable? And what things about me have ever hurt you or made you disappointed? i don't want you to answer just to make me feel better. i want your honest thoughts. i believe that sometimes we cannot see ourselves clearly until someone else shows us a different perspective. So i want to use your answer as a way to reflect, learn, and understand myself better. this is not about defending myself or arguing about who is right or wrong. i just want to know how you see me. ♡ i made all of this so you wouldn't have to get tired of reading my long chats. I know you prefer getting straight to the point. But what can i do? Everything i wrote here feels important to me. 😭 Sometimes, words can be misunderstood or taken the wrong way. i just hope that one day i'll be able to see you in real life instead of only through a screen. Thank you for taking the time to read everything. With love, Rizya ❤️
